Meet Dr. Vishu Jain

Consultant - GI, HPB & Minimal Access Surgery | Bansal Hospital, Bhopal

Dr. Vishu Jain, an expert in gastrointestinal and hepato-pancreato-biliary surgery, completed his MBBS from Maulana Azad Medical College, MS in General Surgery from PGIMER Chandigarh, and MCh in Surgical Gastroenterology from AIIMS Jodhpur. He is trained in advanced laparoscopy, robotic surgery, and liver transplantation. His expertise spans complex surgeries for cancers of the esophagus, stomach, liver, pancreas, and colon, as well as minimally invasive treatments for gallstone disease and hernias.

An active member of NAMS, ASI, IASG, and AMASI, Dr. Jain is also a Fellow of IAGES and Associate Fellow of the American College of Surgeons (ACS).

He has published research in international journals and is currently practicing at Bansal Hospital, Bhopal.
