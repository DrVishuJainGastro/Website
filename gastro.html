Gastro


Gastrointestinal (GI) disorders can significantly impact your quality of life. Dr. Vishu Jain provides expert diagnosis and treatment for various GI conditions, including those affecting the esophagus, stomach, gallbladder, pancreas, and liver.

Conditions Treated

  • Esophageal Disorders: Acid reflux, Barrett's esophagus, esophageal cancer, achalasia, esophageal diverticula.

  • Gastric Disorders: Gastric ulcers, gastritis, gastric cancer, gastroparesis, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).

  • Gallbladder Disorders: Gallstones, cholecystitis, gallbladder cancer.

  • Pancreatic Disorders: Pancreatitis, pancreatic cancer, pancreatic cysts

  • Liver Disorders: Cirrhosis, hepatitis, liver cancer, fatty liver disease


Symptoms to Look Out For

Esophagus
  • Heartburn
  • Chest Pain
  • Difficulty Swallowing
  • Persistent Cough


Stomach
  • Abdominal Pain
  • Bloating
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Loss of Appetite

Gallbladder
  • Abdominal Pain, especially after eating fatty foods
  • Jaundice
  • Fever
  • Vomiting

Pancreas
  • Heartburn
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Weight loss
  • Jaundice
Liver
  • Fatigue
  • Jaundice
  • Abdominal Swelling
  • Confusion
  • Bleeding

Our Approach

Dr. Jain utilizes the latest techniques and technologies to diagnose and treat gastrointestinal diseases. This includes:

  • Advanced Laparoscopic Surgery: Minimally invasive procedures for conditions like gallstones, hernias, and certain cancers.

  • Robotic Surgery: Precision and control for complex surgeries, often resulting in faster recovery.

  • Liver Transplantation: Restoring liver function for patients with end-stage liver disease.
