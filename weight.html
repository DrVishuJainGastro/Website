Weight Loss Surgery


Welcome to our weight loss surgery page. Our experienced gastro doctor specializes in providing safe and effective surgical solutions for individuals struggling with obesity. With a focus on patient-centered care and advanced surgical techniques, we are committed to helping you achieve your weight loss goals and improve your overall health.

How We Treat Weight Loss Surgery

Dr. Jain offers a variety of weight loss surgery procedures, including:

  • Gastric bypass : This procedure involves creating a smaller stomach pouch and bypassing a portion of the small intestine.

  • Sleeve gastrectomy : This procedure involves removing a large portion of the stomach, leaving behind a smaller, tube-shaped stomach

  • Gastric banding : This procedure involves placing a band around the upper part of the stomach to restrict food intake.

Our doctor will carefully evaluate your individual needs and recommend the most appropriate weight loss surgery procedure for you.


Symptoms to Look Out For

When to Consider Weight Loss Surgery:

If you are struggling with obesity and have tried non-surgical weight loss methods without success, you may be a candidate for weight loss surgery. Some common symptoms of obesity include:

  • Difficulty losing weight or maintaining weight loss
  • Health conditions related to obesity, such as diabetes, high blood pressure, or heart disease
  • Sleep apnea
  • Joint pain
  • Low self-esteem or depression
