Dr. Vishu Jain is a qualified and expert surgeon specializing in gastrointestinal, hepato-pancreato-biliary, and minimal access surgery.

His journey into medicine began with an impressive AIR 45 in AIPMT 2010, leading him to earn his MBBS from the esteemed Maulana Azad Medical College, New Delhi. He continued his academic excellence by securing AIR 45 in the postgraduate entrance exam, completing his MS in General Surgery at PGIMER, Chandigarh.

During his residency, Dr. Jain developed a deep passion for managing gastrointestinal conditions. This drive led him to rank AIR 5 in the AIIMS superspeciality entrance exam, where he pursued an MCh in Surgical Gastroenterology at AIIMS Jodhpur, training under Dr. Vaibhav Kumar Varshney. His extensive training encompasses advanced laparoscopic techniques, robotic surgery, and liver transplantation.

Dr. Jain specializes in performing complex and minimally invasive surgeries for cancers of the esophagus, stomach, liver, gallbladder, pancreas, colon, and rectum. He also has significant expertise in treating gallstone disease and hernias (inguinal, ventral, and hiatal) using laparoscopic methods.

His skill set extends to complex procedures such as esophageal replacement for corrosive esophagus and surgeries for portal hypertension.

Currently, Dr. Jain serves as a Consultant in Gastrointestinal, Hepato-pancreato-biliary, and Minimal Access Surgery at Bansal Hospital, Bhopal, where he continues to make a profound impact on the treatment of GI-related diseases.
